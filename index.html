Who we are

Patrick Swanson and Kaveh Waddell have spent the year at Stanford studying and prototyping generative AI tools and strategy for newsrooms as John S. Knight journalism fellows.

Patrick came to Palo Alto from Vienna, where he founded and led the social media team in Austria's largest newsroom. Kaveh is an investigative data journalist based in San Francisco; he has reported on AI, privacy and society for Consumer Reports, Axios and The Atlantic.
