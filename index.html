Verso is an AI consulting lab based in San Francisco.

We draw on journalistic and technical experience to help newsrooms, nonprofits, and business leaders step into the AI age.

AI literacy training

Verso provides customized workshops that help organizations use AI to become more creative, sustainable and effective. Our training draws on techniques such as user-centered design, rapid prototyping and experiential learning to introduce participants to the possibilities and risks of AI.

AI business strategy

Verso collaborates with organizational leaders to develop ethical and effective AI strategies. We facilitate internal conversations to draw out organizational values, then work with leaders to communicate these principles publicly in order to build trust with audiences and membership.

AI product design

Verso helps organizations identify areas of opportunity for using AI, and either points leaders toward existing solutions or creates prototype AI tools to solve the specific problems. We work with organizations to test and refine these tools, and to develop strategies for integrating them into existing workflows.

Who we are

Patrick Swanson and Kaveh Waddell are journalists and technologists. They were 2024 John S. Knight Fellows at Stanford University, where they studied and designed generative AI tools and strategy for newsrooms.

Patrick founded and led the social media team in Austria's largest newsroom, reaching a daily audience of 2.4 million on TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook. Kaveh is an investigative data journalist based in San Francisco; he has reported on AI, privacy and society for Consumer Reports, Axios and The Atlantic.
